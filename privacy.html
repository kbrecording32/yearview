Privacy Policy for Year View

Last updated: December 30, 2024

TL;DR: Year View does not collect, store, or share any of your personal data. Everything stays on your device.

Introduction

Year View ("we", "our", or "us") operates the Year View mobile application. This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal data when you use our app.

Information Collection and Use

We do not collect any personal information.

Year View is designed with privacy in mind. The app:

  • Does not collect any personal data
  • Does not track your usage
  • Does not use analytics services
  • Does not require account creation
  • Does not connect to external servers

Local Data Storage

Year View stores your theme preferences locally on your device using iOS App Groups. This data:

  • Never leaves your device
  • Is not backed up to our servers
  • Is only used to remember your color theme selection
  • Can be deleted by uninstalling the app

Third-Party Services

Year View does not use any third-party services, analytics, or advertising frameworks.

Children's Privacy

Our app does not collect any information from anyone, including children under 13.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.
